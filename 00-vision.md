# Vision

## Product Vision
Probable is the "Stripe for Prediction Markets". We provide the underlying financial infrastructure, order routing, wallet abstraction, compliance layer, and embeddable UI widgets to enable any developer to integrate prediction markets into their product with a single API.

## Goals
1. **Developer First**: Enable market creation, trading, and settlement in under 15 lines of code.
2. **Atomic Settlement**: Achieve sub-second execution and trade resolution with Polymarket.
3. **Seamless Onboarding**: Eliminate the web3 friction by offering embedded gasless wallets (smart accounts) for users.

## Target Personas
1. **Fintech Apps**: Seeking to offer event-based betting or hedging features.
2. **Media & News Outlets**: Wishing to embed interactive prediction polls with real-time financial stakes.
3. **Web3 Platforms**: Needing direct API-based programmatic trading on prediction markets.

## North Star Metrics
* **Volume Processed (GMV)**: Total USD volume transacted through Probable API.
* **Developer Activation Time**: Time taken from sign-up to first API market trade.
* **API Latency**: Median time to stream quotes and execute orders.
